Product generation and visualization

Accumulation of a specific product is triggered by pushing one of seven buttons at the bottom of the main window. This transparently generates a specific product data set with a default name which is subsequently displayed with either imgdisplay for images or dsplot for histograms, spectra, and rate curves. No display tool is by default invoked on filtered tables and when you click on Fix Expression button.
