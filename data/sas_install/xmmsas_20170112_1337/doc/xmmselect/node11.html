Selections from displayed products

Once a product is displayed the user can interactively define one (spectra/histograms and rate curves) or two-dimensional (images) regions with the aid of the mouse and make xmmselect transform these into a boolean selection expression for a subsequent new product extraction.

Subsections
  • Spatial - 2D regions from imgdisplay or dsplot
  • Others - 1D regions from dsplot
