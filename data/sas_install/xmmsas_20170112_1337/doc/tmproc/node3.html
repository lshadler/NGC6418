Description

tmproc will open the required raw TM datafiles, and for those DU (NCTRS frames) with the time stamp within the entered time range,
- Datafiles info will be displayed (verbosity 4, sparse)
- DU and Frames statistics will be displayed (verbosity 5, verbose)
- Packet statistics will be displayed (verbosity 6, noisy).
- Packet dumps will be generated (verbosity 7).

In principle, both VC0 and VC7 will be dumped.

Future devleopments will allow basic fixing process,
- Remove bad data periods (frame based).
- Modify information about frames and packets (beware the checksums).
