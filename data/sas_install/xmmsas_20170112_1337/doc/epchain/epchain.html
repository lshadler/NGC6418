epchain

Abstract:

The task epchain generates the EPIC-PN event list product making use of the tasks atthkgen, epframes, badpixfind, badpix, epreject, epnoise, epevents, attcalc, evlistcomb, epfast, epexposure, epatplot, tabgtigen, and evselect.
