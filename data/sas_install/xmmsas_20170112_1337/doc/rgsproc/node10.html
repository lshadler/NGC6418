rgsframes

rgsframes creates the intermediate event lists and adds to them the PIXELS and EXPOSURE tables, and an internally generated GTI table called STDGTI. The ODF data are reformatted to some extent, and tested for faults and inconsistencies. The GTI table created here sets the most relaxed standard for what time intervals may be included in the data products, and subsequent stages can only act to further restrict these intervals.
